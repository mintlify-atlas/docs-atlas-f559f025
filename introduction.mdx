## Why KillerPDF?

Most PDF tools are either bloated subscriptions, cloud-dependent, or ad-riddled rebrands of the same engine. KillerPDF is different:

- **Truly portable** — one EXE, ~6 MB zipped. No installer, no admin rights, no config files
- **No account required** — open the app and start working
- **No telemetry** — nothing phones home, ever
- **GPLv3 open source** — full source available; forks must stay open

## Get started in 30 seconds

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download KillerPDF">
    Grab the latest ZIP from [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/SteveTheKiller/KillerPDF/releases/latest/download/KillerPDF.zip). No installer needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Extract and run">
    Unzip anywhere — your Desktop, a USB drive, a network share. Double-click `KillerPDF.exe`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open a PDF">
    Drag a PDF onto the drop zone, or click **Open** in the toolbar and pick your file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start editing">
    Use the toolbar to pick a tool — highlight, draw, add text, or sign. Click **Save As** when you're done.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  KillerPDF requires Windows 10 or 11 (64-bit). No .NET runtime installation is needed — everything is bundled inside the EXE.
</Note>
